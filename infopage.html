Guided Workflow

We began with the idea of creating a tool that would allow users to create a simple menu from scratch. What we really wanted was for people to be able to do that without even thinking about it.

We understood that some users might need to be guided through exactly what a menu should have on it and the ability to change those elements to their required style and in variation.



Seemless Design Principles

Our idea brought us closer to the idea that we needed a tool that followed a single, simple stream of logic that would encompass the entire process for making menus. So, we created a tool that demonstrates that in a quick and user friendly manor.



Idea Behind Minu

We first wanted to a create platform that could easily manage the entire process of finding a menu online, and communicating that from restaurant owners and chefs. Then we decided that an even more urgent issue existed...

New business owners, chefs and experienced cooks would have to outsource a lot of their design when it came to creating a quick menu or template for their restaurant.
